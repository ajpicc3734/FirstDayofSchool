What We'll be Learning

  • Welcome to AP Computer Science Principles. This course will focus on the foundational concepts of computer science and explore the impact computing and technology have on our society.
    This course has a unique focus on creative problem solving and real-world applications, this course will give you the opportunity to explore several important topics of computing using your own ideas and creativity, use the power of computing to create artifacts of personal value, and develop an interest in computer science that will hopefully foster further endeavors in the field.
    Prerequisites: There are no official prerequisites for this course. This course does not require any computer programming experience.

    • Topics

  • We will be programming with JavaScript, topics include declaring variables, APIs (application programming interface), if/else statements, for loops, and much more!
